About the Program

The E-Waste Recycling Campus Expert program is designed to create a network of student leaders who champion responsible electronic waste management at their educational institutions. As a Campus Expert, you'll receive training, resources, and support to implement e-waste collection drives, educational workshops, and awareness campaigns.

Program Benefits

Professional Development

Gain valuable leadership, project management, and communication skills that will enhance your resume.

Networking Opportunities

Connect with sustainability professionals and like-minded students across the country.

Exclusive Resources

Access to e-waste collection equipment, educational materials, and digital tools.

Earn Rewards

Eligible for grants up to 300 points each year to implement campus e-waste initiatives.

How to Apply

Follow these steps to become an E-Waste Recycling Campus Expert:

1

Check Eligibility Requirements

To qualify, you must be:

  • A current student at an accredited college or university
  • In good academic standing (minimum GPA of 2.5)
  • Committed to serving as a campus expert for at least one academic year
  • Able to dedicate 3-5 hours per week to program activities
  • Passionate about sustainability and waste reduction
2

Complete the Online Application Form

Fill out the application form below with your personal information, academic background, and responses to the short-answer questions about your interest in e-waste management.

Interview Process

Selected candidates will be invited for a 30-minute virtual interview with program coordinators. The interview will assess your passion for sustainability, leadership capabilities, and specific ideas for your campus.
